Player Agreement — Read Carefully

The Rules Of
The Camp.

Every player signs the same agreement before the first night. It is short. It is final. Nobody has ever asked for a copy to keep.

  • Players cannot stop playing

    Once a game begins, it continues until a winner is decided. There is no forfeit option after the first whistle.

  • A player who refuses is eliminated

    Sitting out is treated the same as losing. The number on your chest is called either way.

  • Games may end early by majority vote

    If more than half the remaining players agree, the round — and the tournament — can be called off entirely.

  • The prize grows with every elimination

    Every player removed adds to the total above the dormitory. Everyone can see the number change in real time.

  • One winner, six games, no exceptions

    The tournament does not pause for injury, weather, or appeals. The schedule has never changed in any season.
